About

We design sustainability that feels effortless

IRPAST started with a simple idea: the tools people use every day should support the planet without compromising on quality, beauty, or ease.

Our story, mission, and vision

We blend clean industrial design with responsible sourcing to create products that fit naturally into homes, teams, and communities that care about their footprint.

  • Company story: born from climate-focused product research and a belief that better design can shift better habits.
  • Mission: help people adopt greener systems through products that are intuitive, durable, and desirable.
  • Vision: build a future where sustainable technology feels like the obvious default, not a compromise.
